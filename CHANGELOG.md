## [1.0.0] — 2026-06-18

### Added
- Initial release of WorldView
- Real-time global intelligence dashboard with 500+ curated news feeds
- Dual map engine: 3D globe (globe.gl) and WebGL flat map (deck.gl) with 56 layer types
- Country Instability Index (CII) v8 scoring for 31 Tier-1 countries
- Finance radar: 29 stock exchanges, commodities, crypto
- 6 site variants: full, tech, finance, commodity, happy, energy
- 24 language support with RTL
- Tauri 2 native desktop app (macOS, Windows, Linux)
- Local AI support via Ollama
- 65+ external data source integrations
